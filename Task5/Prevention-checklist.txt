STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

* Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
* Always verify payment requests before approving
* Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
* Use only official and trusted payment apps
* Enable app lock and security features
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
* Regularly check your bank statements
* Report fraud immediately on cybercrime.gov.in
* Call helpline number 1930 in case of fraud
