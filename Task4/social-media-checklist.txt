SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS

DO:

* Use professional and respectful language
* Keep your profile clean and appropriate
* Verify information before sharing
* Protect your privacy settings
* Think before posting anything online

DON’T:

* Share personal or sensitive information
* Post offensive or inappropriate content
* Click on unknown or suspicious links
* Spread fake news or misinformation
* Engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
